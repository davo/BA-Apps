Splash.FM

Splash.FM is a fast, simple, and social music discovery platform that has been generating buzz in both the startup and music worlds. The app shows you what your friends are listening to and helps you find new music that you’ll actually love.

The Problem

Splash.FM’s founders came to MojoTech with an idea: they wanted to make the best social music discovery service out there. The idea was brilliant, but we had to start from nothing to turn the vision into a product that people would use.

The Solution

To turn the site into a destination rather than a background app, we built a UI centered around a real-time social stream to keep users interacting with the product. By making it easy for users to “Splash” their favorite songs, we could use gamification to keep them engaged longer.

The Outcome

Shortly after launch, almost everyone was talking about Splash.FM. In addition to coverage in TechCrunch, GigaOM, and rhythmMobile, Billboard.com called Splash.FM “a Twitter for music”.

What The Press Is Saying.

"Oh, you liked that band since before they were cool? Now you can prove it with Splash.FM"

Savant

Savant is a world leader in home and facilities control, automation, and media software systems. Their platform cohesively addresses control, audio/video, telephony, digital display, energy management, and the media integration needs of today’s most advanced environments.

The Problem.

Savant needed a web-based, real-time dashboard that would allow their users to monitor hundreds of devices and thousands of data points without being overwhelmed by information.

The Solution.

We helped Savant create a customizable and elegant dashboard with support for real-time updates from separate geographic locations with Node.js and Socket.IO.

The Outcome.

Both new and existing customers fell in love with the dashboard immediately. It also garnered rave reviews and awards from industry experts.

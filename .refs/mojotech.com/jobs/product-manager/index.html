We are looking for a Product Manager to join our team in Providence, RI or New York, NY.

Nice to meet you.

MojoTech builds web and mobile apps for big and soon-to-be-big companies. We’re passionate about creating web and mobile apps that win. We turn ideas into products, build full-featured applications, mentor and accelerate development teams, rescue stray projects, and help our clients make smart decisions that pay off. Join us!

Qualifications.

  • Work closely with MojoTech’s clients to understand their product vision, and break it down into an actionable backlog of user stories for the development team.
  • Work hands-on with the development team to prioritize, plan, and deliver software that meets client requirements.
  • Help clients understand our iterative development approach and focus on delivering a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) through careful and deliberate prioritization.
  • Help MojoTech plan resource allocation across multiple projects.

Desired Skills & Character.

  • 3+ years of product or project management experience, especially with web-based products.
  • Skilled at defining and prioritizing product features.
  • Experience working with Agile teams.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • Up on recent movements in product and SaaS development.
  • Curiosity to learn about new methodologies and approaches.
  • Resourceful and self-initiating.
  • Likes fast-paced environments.

Why Us?

  • 401(k) with match
  • Dedicated time for hacking and learning
  • Expenses paid for conference speakers
  • Small, results-driven, pragmatic team
  • Your choice of hardware, OS, editor, etc.
  • Catered lunches / beer club
  • A job you love with a team you can be proud of

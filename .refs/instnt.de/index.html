    We are currently looking for:

    JUNIOR ART DIRECTOR / SCREENDESIGNER

    This position is part of INSTNT, RTT’s creative department. INSTNT works similar to a creative digital agency and aims to create new ways of product experiences, such as interactive and immersive showrooms, which are based on RTT’s technological know–how.

    Developing user experience and film concepts, creativity, interactive applications

    • Affinity for developing interactive design tools and graphical user interfaces
    • Design wireframes and layouts
    • Develop elaborated user experience concepts with a convincing visual look and feel
    • Close collaboration with our Art Directors, Developers and 2D-/3D- Artists
    • Creation and implementation of usability and interaction concepts
    REQUIREMENTS

    Interaction and Screen Design, Photoshop, talent for design

    • Completed vocational training or studies with creative focus
    • Skills in the areas Interaction and Screendesign
    • Thorough understanding of creation, interaction and animation

    • Modern understanding of design and high quality demands
    • English skills written and spoken
    ABOUT US

    We are RTT’s creative department. The name INSTNT expresses our primary creative objective: Creating ideas that ‘instantly’ get hold of a customer and lead to enduring moments of product experience.
    Since RTT’s foundation in 1999, RTT has witnessed impressive growth, with now over 650 employees worldwide across Germany, Belgium, France, Spain, USA, Brazil, Japan, and China. Our success has been driven by our mission to draw people into an active experience of a customer’s brand via interactive and immersive technology. We are the leaders in high-end 3D visualization in addition to developing revolutionary process technology for clients such as Audi, BMW, Volkswagen, Ferrari, Lamborghini, and Adidas.
